garlic pork sausage (or sweet italian) (or brats)
chicken drumsticks
some sort of extra fatty pork like pancetta

see if you can get a dried thyme, rosemary, marjoram, and oregano mix (herbs de provence)
and if not find an economical way to get most of them as fresh (tbh better)

a large carrot
one onion, diced
whole head of garlic (chopped not minced)
a couple tomatoes and tomato paste (tomatoes seeded and rough chopped)
beans (white beans) (cannellini) a big can
-------------



oven to 350

heat pan, the add oil
crisp up the pancetta, then remove
then do whole sausages in that fat (sear, brown)
then sear tf out of the drumsticks to be golden asf then remove
while chicken is searing, coin sausages
sear sausage coins (essentially double searing the sausages)

--------

lower heat on pan, deglaze with white wine
add onion - once started to sweat, add garlic, then herbs minced
once everything is aeromatic, add chopped tomatoes - let water cook out
also add carrotss + add a splash of white wine if not wet enough
then mix in tomato paste like idk a good dollup

add beans with liquid get it all up to temp
transfer to casserole dish or something oven safe
add in all the things set aside

add rosemary sprig and thyme sprigs to the top for aesthetics

roast one hour
